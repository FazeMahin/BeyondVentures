This Year's Theme

We're excited to announce this year's theme:

"From Ideas to Impact: The New Wave of Change Makers"

This theme focuses on the journey of entrepreneurs, innovators, and thought leaders who have taken their ideas and transformed them into impactful solutions. It highlights stories of resilience, creativity, and determination, and aims to inspire students to think critically about how they can contribute to positive change in their communities and the world.

Through this theme, we aim to feature stories of both well-established giants and emerging underdogs who are making a difference, and encourage students to explore how they can be part of this new wave of change-makers.

About Beyond Ventures

Beyond Ventures is Dubai Scholars Private School's first-ever commerce magazine, dedicated to inspiring students by showcasing real-world success stories, innovative business ideas, and thought leadership.

The magazine aims to create a platform where students can learn from industry giants, explore entrepreneurial journeys, and foster creativity, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Through its features, Beyond Ventures encourages students to think critically about global challenges and explore innovative solutions to make a lasting impact on the world.

This Year's Themes

Under our overarching theme "From Ideas to Impact: The New Wave of Change Makers," we have two exciting sub-themes for submissions:

Solving Global Problems with Innovative Ideas

This theme encourages students to explore how creative and impactful ideas can be used to tackle some of the world's most pressing issues. They must link their ideas to one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to show how innovation can contribute to global progress.

Fun and Creative Ideas

This theme focuses on light-hearted, imaginative, and out-of-the-box ideas. Whether it's a new way to engage in sports, a creative social initiative, or an unusual innovation, this theme is all about fun and thinking outside the conventional frameworks. Students are encouraged to think about how ideas can be both entertaining and impactful.

Industry Giants vs. Underdogs

We believe in showcasing diverse journeys to success. While industry giants demonstrate what's possible with persistence and vision, emerging underdogs remind us that innovation can come from anywhere. Both paths offer valuable lessons in entrepreneurship and making an impact.

Submit Your Article

We invite all students to contribute to Beyond Ventures by submitting their articles that align with our themes.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Word Limit: 450 words maximum
  • Plagiarism Policy: All submissions must be original work. Plagiarism will not be tolerated, and any submission found to have more than 10% plagiarized content will be disqualified.
  • Format: Please submit your work in a clear, well-structured format with proper grammar and punctuation.
  • Theme Alignment: Ensure your submission aligns with one of the two themes mentioned above.
  • Citations: If you reference any external sources, please include proper citations.
  • Images: If you include images, ensure they are properly credited and copyright-free.
  • Deadline: Please check the submission form for the current deadline.
